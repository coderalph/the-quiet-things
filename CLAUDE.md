# Project Context

## What this is
A short story collaboration with Ralph, a writer (not a coder). This is his first Claude Code project.

## The Story: "The Quiet Things"
A romantic comedy set in a post-scarcity future (Culture-like setting).

**Premise:** Tomas, a quiet artist who creates immersive experiential pieces, falls for Veda, a sharp and curious woman. He tries to impress her with a grand public gesture, misreads her overwhelmed silence as rejection, and flees across the solar system. She chases him down to ask why he ever thought he lost her.

**Theme:** Love doesn't require grand gestures. She fell for him watching him work quietly, not for spectacle.
